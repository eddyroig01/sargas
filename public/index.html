Critical Challenge

The Sargassum Challenge

Massive sargassum blooms threaten beaches globally, marine ecosystems, and tourism economies. Current solutions are ineffective, costly, and environmentally harmful.

Environmental Impact

Sargassum decomposition depletes oxygen, creates toxic hydrogen sulfide, and destroys marine habitats affecting entire ecosystems.

Economic Damage

Tourism losses exceed $120M annually in the Caribbean as beaches become unusable and resort operations are severely impacted.

Failed Solutions

Traditional beach cleaning is reactive, labor-intensive, and fails to address the root cause of inshore sargassum accumulation.

Revolutionary Solutions

Our Autonomous Fleet

A comprehensive three-part autonomous system that detects, intercepts, and collects sargassum before it reaches shore.

ISAC - AI Collection Platform

Our flagship autonomous watercraft uses advanced AI navigation and high-capacity collection systems to gather and store sargassum efficiently near shore.

  • Solar-boosted autonomous operation
  • Obstacle avoidance with AI navigation
  • Automated bagging and onboard storage
  • Real-time mission control and tracking
  • Operator interface via web application

ISAC Scout - Interceptor Vessel

Compact support craft that prevents blooms from reaching shore by redirecting them into inshore holding zones until ISAC is available. Fully autonomous and fleet-coordinated.

  • Lightweight, energy-efficient design
  • Bloom interception and redirection
  • Shoreline protection during ISAC missions
  • Reactive positioning based on drift patterns
  • Controlled and monitored via web platform

SargaScan - Early Warning AI

Solar-powered camera network uses AI algorithms to detect sargassum and predict its movement. All insights processed through our web platform with real-time data visualization.

  • Computer vision sargassum detection
  • Solar-powered camera networks
  • Predictive drift modeling
  • Real-time alert system
  • Data visualized and stored in web app

Fleet Hub - Web Platform

Centralized command platform managing ISAC, ISAC Scout, and SargaScan fleets. Real-time monitoring, mission coordination, and comprehensive data analytics.

  • Centralized dashboard for all fleet assets
  • Historical mission logs and performance analysis
  • Multi-role access for resorts, governments, and teams
  • Real-time telemetry and status monitoring
  • Mission planning and automated deployment

About SargaSolutions

Innovation & Mission

SARGAS.AI is a maritime technology startup developing autonomous systems to tackle the growing threat of sargassum seaweed along tropical coastlines. Our cutting-edge approach combines robotics, artificial intelligence, and sustainable design principles.

Founded by a team of mechatronics engineers, software developers, and entrepreneurs, we deliver the world's first fully autonomous sargassum management system. Our technology represents a paradigm shift from reactive beach cleaning to proactive offshore intervention.

While our initial mission focuses on protecting the Caribbean's ecological and economic value, our autonomous navigation technologies are designed for broader applications in marine operations, environmental monitoring, and water-based mobility solutions.
